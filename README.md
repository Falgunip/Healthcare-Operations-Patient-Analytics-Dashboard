📌 Overview
This project analyzes patient and operational healthcare data to identify trends, monitor KPIs, and support business decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Clean and validate healthcare datasets
Analyze patient trends and operational metrics
Build dashboards for business insights
Track KPIs such as wait time, revenue, and cancellations

🛠️ Tools Used
SQL (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
Excel / CSV (Data Source)
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

📊 Key Insights
Identified departments generating highest revenue
Analyzed patient visit trends over time
Measured average wait times and operational efficiency
Calculated cancellation rates impacting business
